üìù Science Communication

I enjoy translating complex scientific concepts into accessible content. I write articles for clubs at UCSD, where I work on making research findings understandable for broader audiences. Effective science communication is essential for public health literacy and helps bridge the gap between research and public understanding.

üëï Fast Fashion & Environmental Health

Working on a collaborative research study examining the environmental and health impacts of fast fashion among San Diego students. Our team is targeting pediatricians to raise awareness about textile-related health concerns, particularly focusing on how microplastics and chemical exposures from synthetic clothing affect children's health. We are presenting our findings as a poster at an upcoming conference. This interdisciplinary project combines public health policy, environmental science, and community outreach.

üåç Global Health Policy

I'm deeply interested in international health governance and have written analytical pieces on PEPFAR policies and global health initiatives. Understanding how policy decisions affect healthcare delivery on a global scale is crucial to creating equitable health systems.

üß¨ Oncology Research

I'm passionate about cancer research and aspire to become an oncologist who views healthcare as both science and advocacy. I've written about Notch signaling pathways and their implications in cancer treatment, exploring how these molecular mechanisms can inform therapeutic approaches.

ü¶† Microbiomes in Medicine

The overall neglect of microbiomes in medicine until recently means we have lots to rediscover. I'm fascinated by the microbiome-cancer relationship and how understanding these complex ecosystems can lead to new treatment approaches and insights into disease prevention.
